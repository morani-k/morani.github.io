About

Welcome to my portfolio! I am a painter based in Busan, South Korea. My work brings the timeless beauty of traditional Korean art with a modern perspective. Through my paintings, I seek to showcase how old art forms possess a unique sense of peace and beauty, offering a refreshing contrast to contemporary works.
